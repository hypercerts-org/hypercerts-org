### Overview

Your display name and image represent you on hyperboards that include hypercerts you own. Updating this information is simple and helps personalize your presence on the platform.

### Accessing the Settings Page

To adjust your display image or name:
1. Navigate to the top-right corner of the app interface.
2. Click on your wallet address (e.g., `0x58CC...5EB0`) to open the dropdown menu.
3. Select **Settings** from the menu.

### Updating Your Display Name and Image

On the **Settings** page, you'll find the following fields:

#### 1. **Display Name**
- Enter a name of your choice in the **Display Name** field.
- **Character Limit:** Max 30 characters.

#### 2. **Image**
- Paste the URL of an image you want to use in the **Image** field.
- Ensure the image URL is valid and publicly accessible.

### Saving Your Changes

Once you've updated your display name and/or image:
1. Click the **Save changes** button.
2. Your updates will be reflected on all hyperboards where your hypercerts are displayed.
